Continuous Controls Monitoring (CCM) Rule Definitions

T1 Split POs: ≥ 3 transactions for same vendor in 7 days, each under the $10,000 limit but summing to ≥ $10,000.

T2 Round Invoices: Invoice amount has a round integer value (multiples of $5k) and is ≥ $5,000.

T3 Out-of-Hours: Transactions approved during weekends (Sat/Sun) or late night hours (18:00 to 07:59).

T4 Segregation of Duties: Invoices approved by the exact same user ID that raised the Purchase Order (PO).

T5 Exact Duplicates: Same vendor ID + same amount + same invoice number (identifies double processing).

T6 Fuzzy Duplicates: Same vendor + amount within ±3 days OR invoice numbers differing by exactly 1 character.

T7 Broken 3-Way Match: Invoices processed directly for payment without a valid PO reference number.

T8 Non-Master Vendor: Payments issued to vendor IDs not registered in the approved Vendor Master Directory.

T9 Bank Detail Change: Invoice paid to account numbers differing from the Master registered routing info.

T10 Sequential Invoices: ≥ 3 sequential invoice IDs from same vendor in close dates (potential invoice splitting).

* Note: For Split POs (T1) and Sequential Invoices (T10), multiple separate cases might merge into a single audit exception if their transaction dates happen to fall in the same rolling windows. This explains why "cases detected" can sometimes be lower than "cases planted", simulating realistic cluster consolidation.
