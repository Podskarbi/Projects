EXECUTIVE SUMMARY  3  The Office of Internal Audit and Investigations (OIAI) conducted an audit of the   UNICEF   Egypt  C ountry   O ffice , covering the period from   January 2023   to   July 2024 . The audit was conducted  from   7   to   21 July 2024   in accordance with   the Code of Ethics and   the International Standards for  the Professional Practice of Internal Auditing.   The overarching objective of the audit was to assess  the adequacy and effectiveness of the governance, risk management and control processes  across   a selection of significant risk a reas of the   c ountry   o ffice   (CO) , including   the overall   control  and   operating   environment, programme management,   supply management,   cash transfers to  implementing partners ,   and harmonized approach to cash transfer   (HACT)   assurance activities . The   UNICEF   Egypt   C ountry   O ffice   supports the Government   of Egypt   in upholding children's  rights, women's rights, and the rights of persons with disabilities, in line with   local and global  commitments.
